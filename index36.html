🍳 Professional Culinary Arts

The culinary world is more than just recipes; it's a multi-billion dollar industry requiring precision, creativity, and business logic. In the EduHub track, you don't just learn to cook; you learn to create a brand.

Pastry & Baking Science

Master the delicate chemistry of fermentation, sugar work, and chocolate tempering. We focus on high-end boulangerie techniques used in Michelin-star bakeries and large-scale industrial production.

Global Gastronomy

Study the cultural roots and modern applications of Mediterranean, Asian, and Latin American cuisines. Learn ingredient sourcing, authentic flavoring, and contemporary fusion presentation.

Nutrition & Specialized Diets

Develop menus for the growing health sector, including keto, vegan, and diabetic-friendly cuisines. This field bridges the gap between a chef and a healthcare consultant.

Restaurant Operations

Learn the "back of house" business: inventory management, food safety standards (HACCP), staff leadership, and maximizing profit margins per plate.

⚽ Sports, Fitness & Management

The sports industry offers diverse career paths beyond the field. We focus on the science of human performance and the business of athletic entertainment.

High-Performance Coaching

Understand bio-mechanics, tactical software, and psychological profiling. Learn how to design training cycles that peak an athlete's performance for major competitions.

Sports Medicine & Recovery

A deep dive into kinesiotherapy, injury prevention, and rapid recovery technologies. This path prepares you for roles in professional clubs or private rehabilitation clinics.

Sports Marketing & Agency

Master the art of athlete branding, sponsorship negotiation, and digital fan engagement. Learn how to manage the "business side" of a sports star's career.

Facility & Event Logistics

From local tournaments to massive stadiums, learn the logistics of crowd control, safety protocols, and commercializing venue space for maximum ROI.
