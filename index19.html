Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduHub?

+

EduHub is a comprehensive global education platform designed to empower students with access to international study opportunities, scholarships, and career development resources. Our mission is to make world-class education accessible to all aspiring learners.

How do I get started with EduHub?

+

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete your profile, and start exploring opportunities. You can browse scholarships, discover study abroad programs, and apply to opportunities that match your academic goals and interests.

Is EduHub free to use?

+

Yes! Browsing and applying for scholarships through EduHub is completely free. We believe education should be accessible without financial barriers to entry.

Which countries do you cover?

+

EduHub partners with universities and scholarship providers across 50+ countries worldwide, including the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Japan, Singapore, and many more. We continue to expand our network regularly.

How do I create an EduHub account?

+

Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. Fill in your basic profile information, and you're ready to go!

How do I reset my password?

+

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I update my profile?

+

Log in to your account and go to "My Profile". You can update your academic information, career interests, language preferences, and other details. Changes are saved automatically.

How do I delete my account?

+

Go to Account Settings, scroll to "Danger Zone", and click "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.

What types of scholarships are available?

+

We offer various scholarship types including merit-based scholarships, need-based scholarships, subject-specific scholarships, country-specific scholarships, and full/partial tuition scholarships. Filter by type to find the best match for you.

How much can I receive?

+

Scholarship amounts vary widely, from partial tuition coverage to full scholarships including accommodation and living expenses. Check individual scholarship details to see what's offered.

Who is eligible for scholarships?

+

Eligibility varies by scholarship. Common requirements include maintaining a minimum GPA, demonstrating financial need, meeting English language requirements, and citizenship criteria. Check each scholarship's eligibility before applying.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

+

Absolutely! We encourage you to apply for multiple scholarships that match your profile. Each application is independent, so applying for one doesn't affect your chances for others.

What documents do I need to apply?

+

Generally, you'll need academic transcripts, a valid passport, proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL), letters of recommendation, a personal statement, and sometimes standardized test scores. Specific requirements vary by scholarship.

How long does the application process take?

+

The application process typically takes 2-6 weeks, depending on the scholarship provider. You'll receive status updates via email throughout the process.

Can I save my application and finish it later?

+

Yes! Your application is automatically saved as you fill it out. You can log back in anytime to continue where you left off, as long as the deadline hasn't passed.

When will I hear about my application status?

+

Check your application status anytime by logging into your account and viewing "My Applications". You'll also receive email notifications about major status changes.

Is there a fee to apply through EduHub?

+

No! EduHub doesn't charge any application fees. However, some universities or scholarship providers may have their own application fees, which you'll pay directly to them.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Since EduHub applications are free, refund policies depend on what you're paying for (premium features, certification courses, etc.). Check our refund policy page for details.

Is my payment information safe?

+

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to protect all payment information. Your data is never stored in plain text.
