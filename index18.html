❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for the scholarship?

▼
Students from any country can apply if they meet our academic requirements (minimum GPA 3.0) and are accepted to a partner university. You must be at least 18 years old and have completed secondary education.

What are the application deadlines?

▼
We accept applications year-round with rolling admissions. However, we recommend applying at least 6-8 months before your intended start date for faster processing and better placement options.

How long does the application process take?

▼
The entire process typically takes 45-60 days from submission to final decision. This includes document review, interviews, and verification of credentials.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

▼
Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarship types and destinations. However, you can only accept one full scholarship. You may combine partial scholarships with other funding sources.

What if my application is rejected?

▼
Rejection is not final. You can reapply after improving your academic record or submitting additional documents. We also offer alternative partial scholarships if you're close to qualifying for a full award.

Is there financial support for living expenses?

▼
Full ride scholarships cover tuition, accommodation, and living allowances. Partial scholarships cover tuition only. All scholarship recipients receive monthly stipends for additional expenses.

What languages must the application be in?

▼
Applications can be submitted in English, French, Kinyarwanda, Kiswahili, or Portuguese. All supporting documents must be officially translated into English.
