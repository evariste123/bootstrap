📋 Requirements & Process

General Requirements

Academic Records

Minimum GPA of 3.0 and official transcripts from your current institution.

Language Proficiency

English proficiency test scores (TOEFL/IELTS) or language certificate if required.

Valid Passport

Passport valid for at least 12 months from your intended arrival date.

Financial Documentation

Bank statements proving ability to cover tuition and living expenses.

Statement of Purpose

Essay explaining your academic goals and reasons for studying abroad.

Recommendation Letters

2-3 letters from professors or employers supporting your application.
