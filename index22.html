❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for jobs through EduHub?

Create a profile, upload your resume, and browse thousands of international job listings. You can apply directly through our platform.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive visa guidance for all major destinations. Our partners include immigration experts who can help with your application.

Are there any language requirements?

Requirements vary by country and position. Most English-speaking countries require IELTS/TOEFL. Some countries like Germany may require basic local language skills.

How long does the hiring process take?

Typically 2-4 months from application to job offer, plus 1-3 months for visa processing depending on the country.
