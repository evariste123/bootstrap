1. The Box Model

margin

Values: top right bottom left | auto | px | rem | %

Creates space outside the element's border.

Margin Area

padding

Values: px | em | % | inherit

Creates space inside the element, between content and border.

Content

box-sizing

Values: content-box | border-box

Determines if padding/border are included in width.
